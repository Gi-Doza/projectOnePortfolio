Gian Mendoza

I’m a student at San Francisco State University, currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Visual Communication with a focus on UX/UI design. Although I am still building experience in the field, I am passionate about applying my skills to real-world projects. Right now, I’m working on designing a website for my local church, which allows me to practice and refine my abilities while learning more about the process of web design.

I have a solid foundation in design tools such as Figma and Webflow, along with basic knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. My primary interest lies in solving problems through technology and design. I find it exciting to research various challenges and come up with creative, user-centered solutions that not only look good but also work seamlessly.

Currently, my main goal is to expand my portfolio by taking on more projects that push me to grow and explore new areas of UX/UI design. I pride myself on my ability to learn quickly and adapt to new challenges, and I have strong organizational skills that help me stay on track with my work. I’m excited to continue developing my skills and contribute to innovative design projects in the future.
